Who Is Donie Burch From Dancing Queens? Wikipedia And Age

Donie Burch is a New York City-based dancer. The precise information about her age has not been revealed. However, looking at her pictures, we assume Burch is not in her thirties.

In her Instagram bio, she introduced herself as a Texan, aunt, lover of all Mexican foods, living and dancing in New York City.

According to her Bravo TV bio, she has been dancing her entire life. The passionate dancer even earned her degree in dance performance.

Iliah Vinikovskiy is Donie Burch’s dance partner. They are determined to show the judges and audience that Donie is one of the best dancers in the nation.

Donie might not have the typical dancer’s physique. However, her skill will surprise people. But the skilled dancer fears judges will rate her based on her appearance more than her ability.

Donie relocated to New York City soon after graduating from college to pursue a career as a jazz or modern dancer.

Still, her job as a buyer quickly blossomed, shattering her ambitions of becoming a successful dancer. But she is now pursuing her dream.

Donie Burch Is A Graduate Of Southern Methodist University

The Dancing Queens contestant completed her high school education at Kingwood High School in 1996.

After finishing her former education, she studied dance at Southern Methodist University and graduated in 1999. The reality TV show star former worked as a buyer at Bergdorf Goodman in New York. She is also a former buyer at Bluefly.

In addition, she worked as a design merchandise coordinator at Uniqio between October 2005 to March 2006. From April 2007 to January 2014, the talented dancer was a buyer, accessories & shoe designer at Tootsies.

Besides participating in the dance competition show, Burch is also the director of retail for Broadway Dance Center. 

Burch has to juggle a busy life – a demanding professional schedule and many obligations to manage.

Donie pushes herself and her body by taking ballroom dancing lessons and pursuing her passion. Still, more importantly, she feeds and satisfies her love for performing and self-expression.

Burch said that dance makes her feel like she is living her fullest life. We wish the talented dancer good luck in her upcoming endeavors.

Who Is Donie Burch Partner/Boyfriend?

The talented dancer Donie Burch is currently single.

Her obsession with dance and her career leaves her with little room for her love life and finding a partner off the dance floor.

However, the dancer appears to be someone who values relationships. She often shares pictures of herself with her family.

Thus, anyone who dates Donie Burch will be a lucky person. She is focusing on her career and is determined to shine bright.
