The rumor that she is pregnant for the fourth time is circulating, but the truth is that she is not.

Are Fox News Anchor Julie Banderas and Husband Andrew Sansone Pregnant In 2022?

Julie Banderas and her husband Andrew Sansone are not pregnant as of today, in the year 2022; so, reports of her pregnancy are only rumors, as they often are with celebrities.

The Fox News Channel reporter last fell pregnant seven years ago, in 2015, when she gave birth to her son. She does share adorable images of her children on social media.

Meanwhile, our sources are still trying to figure out why the pregnancy rumors started in the first place.

Julie is already a working mother to her three wonderful and energetic kids. Her two daughters, Avery and Addison, and a son are her young children.

They are said to be highly supportive of their working mother and make the most of her time when she returns home.

Julie Banderas Baby Bump Photos

Julie Banderas is the mother of three children and is not expecting a fourth, even though she was pregnant before the baby bump photo was taken.

Julie Banderas Family

Julie Banderas’s parents are Fabiola R. and Howard Dexter Bidwell.

She has a sister and four half-siblings from her father’s first marriage.

On August 29, 2009, Julie married Andrew J. Sansone at Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church.

Even though they both work and have three children, they have a happy marriage.

Andrew, her husband, is a member of Habitat for Humanity’s board of directors and the founder of both Old Rock Media and Big Apple Channel.
