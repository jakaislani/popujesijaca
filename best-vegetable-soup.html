Best Vegetable Soup to Buy [Canned Vegetable Soup Taste Test]

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The best vegetable soup is savory, salty, and chock-full of vegetables, of course! Good canned vegetable soup can be hard to find, though. I don’t even typically like vegetable soup, despite being an absolute freak for vegetables. Even the best veggie soup I’ve had has tasted bland and mushy.

The soups listed below, however, are packed with flavor and plenty of toothsome vegetables. They have me rethinking my distaste for this actually tasty soup. Whether you’re looking for something classic or nouveau, this list has the best canned vegetable soup for you. Crack open a can of the best vegetable soup this cold-weather season!

Best Campbell’s

Campbell’s Well Yes! Garden Vegetable Soup With PastaTarget

This is one of the best vegetarian soups out there if you truly love vegetables. There are big pieces of veggies and a broth rich with roasted red pepper and tomato flavor. It’s herby and not too salty and super delicious. The pasta is still al dente, despite living in a can for who knows how long. If you want a truly vegetable-forward canned vegetable soup, this one is for you! There are so many options from Campbells and this is easily our favorite.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

8/10

Best with Pasta

Progresso Vegetable SoupWalmart

Much like in the Campbells Well Yes! Garden Vegetable soup, pasta is a welcome addition to this vegetable soup. I like this classic vegetable soup from Progresso because the vegetables and the pasta are both done well. It’s very mild and has big chunks of veggies floating in the broth. This is the best vegetable soup to have in the pantry if you come down with a cold. It has more oomph than chicken noodle but is still plain enough to soothe an upset stomach or stuffy nose. Plus, you can find Progresso in almost every grocery store and it’s often on sale. A win for affordability.

Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

Rating:

8/10

Best for Veg Lovers

Trader Joe’s Garden Vegetable SoupAffiliate

If you want the best vegetable soup that is heavy on the vegetables, grab a jar of Trader Joe’s Garden Vegetable Soup. It has a great mix of different veggies, making it the best canned vegetable soup for veg fanatics. It’s packed with sweet potatoes, carrots, tomatoes, zucchini, bell peppers, and leafy greens. A bonus feature is that this soup comes in a glass jar instead of a can. The reusability factor makes it easy to store your leftovers. Keep it after you’re done with your soup and you can store anything in it. Tasty and eco-friendly!

Credit: Liv Averett / Trader Joe’s

Rating:

9/10

Best Reduced Sodium

Amy’s Organic French Country Vegetable SoupPavilions

I’ll say what we’re all thinking: All canned vegetable soups should have beans. It makes it so much heartier and more delicious! I love beans. That’s one of the big reasons I loved Amy’s Reduced Sodium French Country Vegetable Soup. It also includes long-grain rice, which makes it so filling and comforting. I could totally see this canned vegetable soup sustaining you as you tend to your sheep in the fields of the French countryside. Please don’t come for me if you’re a French farmer. I’m a comedian and soup lover who lives in L.A. so I’m just taking a guess.

Credit: Liv Averett / Pavillions

Rating:

10/10

Best of the Best

Amy’s Organic Southwestern Fire Roasted Vegetable SoupWalmart

Imagine the best vegetable soup you ever tasted had a baby with a perfectly spiced tortilla soup—that’s Amy’s Southwest Vegetable Soup in a nutshell. What’s not to love? It’s wonderfully seasoned and would be great on its own, but would also be great topped with shredded cheddar, diced raw onion, and a squeeze of fresh lime. You could even scoop it up with some tortilla chips or spoon it over a baked potato. I love a vegetable soup that is versatile. This one isn’t just versatile, it’s bursting with flavor and was the best veggie soup we tried during our taste test.
